ABOUT THE DIRECTOR:

Mr. David Bonilla-Garcia is the Director of Bands at Pottstown High School. He is a graduate of West Chester University with a B.M. in Music Education. From Quakertown, PA, he has worked with many bands in the area and teaches privately as a Trumpet Instructor with Advanced Music Network.

Mr. Bonilla-Garcia marched with The Cadets Drum and Bugle Corps from 2015-2017 and The Cadets2 Drum and Bugle Corps in 2014. He taught High Brass at the Cadets2 in 2018. Playing the trumpet, he has performed with the West Chester Wind Ensemble around the country including the CBDNA (College Band Directors National Association) National Conference in Tempe, AZ and at the New York Wind Band Festival at Carnegie Hall in NYC. A member of Kappa Kappa Psi - National Honorary Band Fraternity, he served as the President of the Eta Rho chapter at West Chester University.

An avid board gamer, he likes to spend his free time around the gaming table with family and friends. Also a creative designer, he enjoys planning marching band and drum corps shows with exciting and unique designs.
