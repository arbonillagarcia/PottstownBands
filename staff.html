Amber Boyer

I started spinning almost 5 years ago (2015) with the Daniel Boone high school marching band. Spun with Daniel Boone high school (2015-2017), Twin Valley highschool (2017-2018), Raiders Drum and Bugle Corps (2018), West Chester University (2019), and Main Line Independent (2019-present) What to know about me I am 19 and am a sophomore psychology student at West Chester University. In 2018 when I moved to a new high school (Twin Valley highschool) without an indoor program I started one for them as a student. As someone who is very passionate about indoor color guard and color guard just in general, know that I and the other staff plan to start an indoor program for Pottstown in the near future. Color guard and band in general have always been a safe place for me. I consider myself to be both a band kid and guard kid as I was a band kid long before I started color guard. The programs I have been a part of throughout the years have made me feel part of something more and I plan to make the students feel the same way about this program.

Lexi

Hey guys!! I'm Lexi!! I am originally from Daniel Boone High School and I am now a senior at West Chester University studying Exercise Science to one day become a Physical Therapist some day! I spun with Daniel Boone for 7 years (middle and high school), spun with an independent colorguard (Classics Colorguard) for 4 years, spun in West Chester University's Golden Ram Marching Band this past fall and have been spinning at a different independent colorguard (Main Line Independent) for 2 years now. So excited to meet you all and have a great season!!

Nick Markellos

Nick graduated from west chester university in january of 2019. While there, he studied classical percussion with Ralph Sorrentino and David Nelson. Nick marched in west chester's band (2015-2017) as well as pursued independent experiences with Imperial Dynasty, Skyliners, United Percussion, and Hawthorne Caballeros both in the battery, and front ensemble.He taught for four seasons at Hempfield High school as a battery tech winning two cavalcade championships and one percussion title. Last winter, Nick was on staff at United Percussion 2 and was slated to be a front ensemble tech for 7th regiment before the 2020 season was canceled
